Introducing Sunlight:


An innovative activity manager, Sunlight revolutionizes task management with seamless efficiency. With its intuitive design and advanced features, Sunlight simplifies your workflow, empowering you to achieve more with less effort.


Key Features:

Voice Commands:
Harness the power of voice commands to effortlessly create new tasks. Simply utter "Sunlight, create a new task," followed by the task details, and Sunlight will swiftly add it to your schedule.

Remove Tasks Easily:
Bid farewell to unnecessary tasks with ease. By commanding "Sunlight, remove recent task," you can promptly eliminate the most recent entry from your agenda, maintaining clarity and focus.

Mic Control:
Take control of your privacy with Sunlight's mic control feature. Simply instruct, "Hey Sunlight, turn off the mic," to deactivate the microphone and ensure your privacy remains intact.

Privacy First:
Sunlight prioritizes your privacy by utilizing your device's microphone responsibly. Rest assured, Sunlight does not compromise your data integrity; your information remains secure and never shared without your consent.

Stay organized effortlessly and enjoy peace of mind with Sunlight's intuitive interface and privacy-centric design.
